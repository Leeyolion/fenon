About Us

Fenon Entertainment is Uganda’s leading 360° event management and audiovisual production company, delivering world-class experiences through creativity, precision, and innovation.

Where It All Began

Established in 2004 as a recording studio, Fenon Entertainment has grown into Uganda’s leading 360° event management and audiovisual production company.
What started as a passion for sound has evolved into a full-scale creative powerhouse—delivering world-class events, film, and production services that connect, inspire, and leave lasting impressions.

Who We Are

At the heart of Fenon is a team of talented creatives, technical experts, and experienced professionals who bring ideas to life.
We are artists, engineers, and visionaries—united by a shared commitment to excellence. Our people are our greatest strength, and their passion and expertise are reflected in every project we deliver.

What We Stand For

Our success is built on attention to detail, precision, and artistic flair. We believe that true excellence lies in the small details—the moments that transform a good experience into an unforgettable one.
We are constantly innovating, learning, and evolving to stay ahead in an ever-changing industry. For us, it’s not just about having the best equipment—it’s about how we use it to create magic.

Our Capability

Over the years, we have invested in a vast inventory of state-of-the-art equipment to support every level of production.
Our capabilities include:
High-resolution indoor and outdoor LED screens
Flying line array sound systems
Staging and rigging solutions
Intelligent and conventional lighting
High-power silent generators
Multi-track audio recording systems
World-class cameras and film equipment
Industry-standard editing platforms
